Privacy Policy for Render Monitor App

Last Updated: September 2025

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at Render Monitor ("we," "us," or "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile app available on iOS.

Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • User identifiers (e.g., User ID)
  • Authentication data (via Firebase Authentication such as Apple Sign-In, Google Sign-In)
  • Subscription status (Pro user or Basic user)
  • Render job metadata (render IDs, status, progress, timestamps)
  • Frame preview images (only for Pro users)

Usage Data:

  • Network connection status
  • Error logs for debugging
  • Interaction with app features and subscription management

Device Information:

  • Device type and operating system version for compatibility optimization

How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you when you create an account or use the app features.
  • Automatically through your use of the app and integrations (e.g., Firebase, RevenueCat).
  • Through backend services, including secure session tokens used for device linking.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain core app functionalities, including render monitoring and frame preview uploads.
  • To manage user authentication and subscription entitlements securely.
  • To communicate updates, respond to support inquiries, and troubleshoot errors.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect the security and integrity of our services.

Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share your data only with trusted third-party services necessary for app operation, including Firebase, RevenueCat, and cloud storage providers. All third parties comply with privacy practices consistent with regulatory requirements.

Data Retention and Deletion

  • We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations.
  • You can request deletion of your account and personal data by contacting support, and we will honor such requests promptly.

User Rights and Controls

  • You may revoke consent for data processing where applicable.
  • You can manage your subscription status through the app or by contacting support.
  • You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data as outlined above.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures such as encrypted communication channels (HTTPS), secure authentication (Firebase Auth), and token-based sessions to protect your data.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the app and updating the effective date.
